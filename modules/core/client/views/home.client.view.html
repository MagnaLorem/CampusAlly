What is Study Swamp?

Study Swamp is an application that allows you to communicate with other students in your classes. To get started, use the Sign Up page to create a Study Swamp account!

How do I find my classes?

Once you have your account and subscribe for the current semester, you'll be able to easily search for your classes on our search page.

What's Next?

Now that you have your classes, just navigate to the chat page to start connecting with your fellow classmates!
